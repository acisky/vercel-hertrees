## Cubinko: A Strategic Puzzle Game

Cubinko is a strategic puzzle game that challenges you to think critically and organize blocks at every turn. This is far from an ordinary block game, breaking away from traditional puzzle rules entirely. Cubinko introduces innovative gameplay mechanics and uniquely iconic blocks that transform each level into a rewarding mental challenge. Sharp logic, solid planning skills, and quick thinking are all essential to succeed in Cubinko. Every single move matters as you progress higher through this captivating puzzle world. You must master strategy if you want to conquer what Cubinko has to offer.

## Innovative Puzzle Gameplay

In Cubinko, you could manipulate blocks that contain up to eight different symbols. The core goal is to place three or more identical blocks adjacent to each other, which eliminates them from the board and opens up new spaces for your subsequent turns.

## Three Game Modes

Cubinko features three distinct game modes designed to test your skills in different ways. Relax Mode is a pressure-free experience where you could immerse yourself in the game without worrying about any limits, and you are free to keep playing as long as there is still room on the board to place the next blocks. Arcade Mode gives you a fixed number of moves, and the goal is to collect the required number of blocks within those moves. Try to optimize each placement and think carefully about your strategy to overcome this challenge in Arcade Mode. Time Attack Mode puts you under the clock, and you must combine as many identical blocks as possible within a limited amount of time. Each mode in Cubinko records your best performance until you break your own record.

## Unique Controls

Cubinko uses simple and intuitive controls. Rotate angle by clicking on the original block. Place blocks on the table by holding down and dragging the mouse.
