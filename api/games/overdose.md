Overdose is a free psychological horror visual novel created by Mooki !! and hosted on itch.io. This is a Ren'Py game where you wake up in a hospital following a suspicious car accident. A mysterious doctor provides your care, but this doctor seems to possess an unsettling amount of knowledge regarding your personal life. You must notice that strange injuries are appearing and memories are vanishing. These unsettling clues suggest the doctor could have been conducting experiments on you long before the current encounter.

## Genre and Gameplay

Overdose combines medical horror, body horror, yandere romance, mystery, and dating sim mechanics. Player choices are central to the narrative and this is how you unlock different outcomes, new CGs, and branching routes. You could customize the main character's name, pronouns, and skin tone to increase immersion.

## Visuals and Content

Overdose uses hand-drawn backgrounds, sprites, and CGs featuring an anime-inspired art style. This demo includes one bad ending, and the game emphasizes that every decision matters. Content warnings are extensive; this includes blood, gore, death, murder, needles, substance abuse, implied physical abuse, screen shaking, and flashing lights.
