## HER TREES : THE PUZZLE HOUSE

HER TREES : THE PUZZLE HOUSE is a distinctive point-and-click puzzle game that challenges you to move, combine, and manipulate objects to solve creative puzzles. This is a game built around discovery — each interaction could reveal a new secret hidden within a peculiar, mysterious room.

This is the second installment in the series, but new players can start playing from here without any prior knowledge or experience.

### Game Features

- HER TREES : THE PUZZLE HOUSE offers a fresh puzzle experience unlike anything found in other games. Every puzzle is designed to be intuitive, so there are no tedious calculations or note-taking required. Players must rely on observation and creativity to progress. Hints and answers are accessible at any time for those who need a little extra guidance. The approximate playtime is around 1 hour.

### Art and Sound

- HER TREES : THE PUZZLE HOUSE presents a dark, storybook-like ambiance that draws you into a surreal dreamworld. The visuals are entirely unique, featuring hand-drawn graphics crafted with meticulous detail. Serene ambient sound accompanies every scene, creating an immersive and atmospheric experience throughout.

### Accessibility

- HER TREES : THE PUZZLE HOUSE is a completely non-verbal game, making it accessible to players of all languages. Since the entire visual style is monochrome, there are no puzzles that rely on color perception. No puzzles involve sound either, so try to enjoy this game in your own way — it could be experienced fully by virtually anyone.
