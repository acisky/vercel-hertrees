Brush Jjaemu is a casual stealth simulation game. This is a title where you use a brush to groom a fluffy cat. The primary goal is to fill the progress bar completely. Your main mission involves brushing the fur without being caught by the angry pet.

## How to Play Brush Jjaemu

### Groom the Cat Safely
Brush Jjaemu requires you to click and hold to start the grooming process on the cat's body. You must watch the movements of the cat very closely during this process. This is where you should stop all actions the moment the cat starts to turn its head. Continuing to brush during a stare will lead to an immediate failure. Patience is the most important skill for a successful groomer in this arena.

### Handle Random Movements
Brush Jjaemu features a cat that does not follow a predictable pattern during the gameplay sessions. You must react to sudden head turns and suspicious glances in real time. This is because the difficulty increases as the cat becomes more alert in later stages. Fast reflexes could allow you to maximize your brushing time during safe windows. You will earn more points by staying calm under the intense gaze of the cat. Try to watch for the ear twitches before the cat turns around. You must stop brushing a split second before the cat fully rotates.

### Game Controls
Controls are simple. Left mouse click to brush. Release to stop.

## Frequently Q&A

### Getting Caught
What happens if the cat catches me? The cat will hiss. Your current grooming session will end immediately.

### Available Cats
Are there different cats to groom? You could unlock various cat breeds and fur colors as you advance through the levels.

### Movement Speed
Does the cat turn around at the same speed every time? No, the movements are random. The cat becomes more alert in later stages.

### Higher Scores
How do I get a higher score? You should try to maintain longer brushing streaks whenever the cat is looking away.
