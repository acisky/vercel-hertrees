Trees Hate You is a fast-paced indie survival game that turns nature into your greatest threat, delivering a chaotic and unforgettable gameplay experience. Players are dropped into a hostile forest where every tree is alive, aggressive, and relentlessly closing in. The core objective of Trees Hate You is straightforward: survive as long as possible while dodging waves of attacking trees that chase, surround, and overwhelm you at every turn.

Trees Hate You stands out from other survival games through its bold creative twist on a familiar genre. Rather than chopping down trees or gathering resources, players must flee from them. This role reversal gives Trees Hate You a fresh and distinctive identity among browser games and indie arcade titles. The gameplay is easy to grasp but hard to master — this is exactly what makes Trees Hate You so compelling for both newcomers and seasoned players alike.

## Key Gameplay Features

**Relentless Survival Action**
Trees Hate You ramps up the difficulty steadily as more trees spawn and close in. Players must stay sharp and keep moving to push their survival time further.

**Accessible and Addictive Controls**
The controls in Trees Hate You are clean and intuitive, letting players start playing without any learning curve. Mastering movement and timing, however, is what separates good runs from great ones.

**Tight Arcade Mechanics**
Every round of Trees Hate You is packed with tension. Quick reactions and smart positioning are must-have skills to avoid getting cornered by the relentless tree swarms.

**High Replay Value**
Trees Hate You is built to keep players coming back. The scoring system could motivate players to beat their own records or compete with others for the top spot.

**A Concept Worth Remembering**
Try to think of another game where trees are the enemy — Trees Hate You owns this idea entirely. This memorable concept makes it one of the most shareable experiences in the indie game market.
