This is a light indie-style game where the narrative follows Bok-su Go, a young woman who gets an invitation to her old college friend Da-jeong Choi's wedding. The story starts with joy and laughter, but as the celebration goes on, Bok-su begins to notice something off. Conversations become unusual, and small details in the ceremony reveal untold stories hidden behind the perfect wedding surface.

## At first, everything seems filled with joy and laughter

However, as the event unfolds, Bok-su senses that something isn’t right. The conversations start to feel a bit strange, and the small things—like a card left on a table or a friend’s casual remark—begin to show the real story behind the beautiful wedding. This is where players must pay close attention to uncover what’s really happening.

## Every action matters in this game

Players can freely move between different wedding areas like the reception hall, the bride’s room, and the photo zone. Each location includes small yet meaningful details, such as a card left on a table, a photo hanging on the wall, or a friend’s casual remark. Try to observe everything closely to see how these pieces fit together.

## By carefully observing and interacting with the environment

You can discover the truth and better understand the special connection between Da-jeong and Bok-su. Must interact with objects and explore every corner to find clues. This is how the game builds its story through exploration and observation.

## Game controls are simple

Must use the mouse to click on objects, choose dialogue options, or move between areas. This is the primary way to interact with the game world.

## Mouse controls

Click to interact with objects, choose dialogue options, or move between areas. This is the standard way to control the game, and it works for nearly every action you need to take.

## WASD or arrow key controls

Move the character with these keys. Could also use the arrow keys if you prefer a more traditional setup.

## Space or enter key controls

Skip dialogue quickly using the Space or Enter key. This is helpful when you want to replay a section or move past a conversation you’ve already seen.
