## Sky Dart Gameplay Guide

Sky Dart challenges you to control the dart and fly the furthest possible distance across the vast sky. Every meter of the flight path tests your perseverance and observation skills. You must stay highly focused to avoid falling to the ground too early. This is a game that delivers an absolute sense of freedom as you watch the dart tear through clouds and soar toward the distant horizon. Try to hit the target boards to maximize your score. Could there be a better way to spend your time?

### Deep-Dive Tactical Guide

In Sky Dart, the first step is the launch phase. You need to click and hold the left mouse button, then pull backward to start accumulating energy. At the same time, moving the mouse gently up or down while holding the button helps you adjust the launch angle. An ideal launch angle is usually around 45 degrees from the ground. This gives the dart both the necessary height and powerful forward momentum. Once the dart leaves the launcher, the game switches to air control mode. You can steer the dart left or right to avoid obstacles like tall trees, large rocks, or small houses. Do not hold and move the mouse too suddenly, as this will cause the dart to lose balance and lose speed quickly. Instead, you must make short and smooth mouse movements to maintain momentum.

### Upgrade System

You use the gold coins you earn to improve stats such as the following:

- Launch power.
- Control ability.
- Income per meter flown.

Investing correctly in these upgrades will help you go further in subsequent turns. Start with control ability to make the dart more responsive.

### How to Shoot

Click on the dart and pull backward to build launch power. Move the mouse left and right to determine the launch direction. While the dart is flying, press and hold the mouse left or right to avoid obstacles and score higher. This is a simple yet effective method.

### Secret Tactics

Focus on upgrading the control stat first. When the dart is more responsive, avoiding obstacles becomes easier. Always look far ahead to predict the location of the next targets. Try to steer the dart straight into the center of the target boards for maximum points. Could these tactics help you set a new record?
