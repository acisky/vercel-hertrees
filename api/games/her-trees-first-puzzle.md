## Her Trees: First Puzzle

Her Trees: First Puzzle is the debut game in the Her Trees series. This series features short point-and-click games focused on discovering hidden codes within a strange, dreamlike world. Her Trees: First Puzzle is playable directly in your browser at no cost, making it an excellent introduction to understand what the rest of the series could offer.

### Gameplay Mechanics

Each puzzle in Her Trees: First Puzzle requires you to uncover a hidden code from a collection of objects. These objects include dice, leaves, branches, and scraps of paper. Some items can be moved around and placed over other objects to reveal clues. The code you must find will always consist of letters ranging from A through I. Your answer can be confirmed by entering it into a keypad located on the side of the screen.

### Puzzle Approach

Her Trees: First Puzzle presents different objects and clever methods to conceal each code. You must treat every puzzle as its own unique challenge. This game has no specific rules for solving these puzzles, but patient observation, methodical searching, and lateral thinking will guide you to the answer. Try to identify patterns shared between objects, as this is likely a productive approach to follow.

### Style and Atmosphere

Her Trees: First Puzzle is in some ways reminiscent of the Rusty Lake series. This game shares a slightly unsettling and surreal visual style, although there are no horror elements to worry about. The lateral-thinking puzzles make Her Trees: First Puzzle an interesting and distinctive entry in the point-and-click puzzle game genre.
