### Control the Launch Power

In Thorn and Balloons, press and hold the screen (or select the thorn ball with your mouse or fingers), then swipe back to control the strength of your launch. The strength of your shot depends on the length you pull. You must shoot at the right strength to bounce the ball effectively. ### Adjust the Launch Angle

Carefully aim the ball in Thorn and Balloons by pulling back in a specific direction. Remember, the ball moves in the opposite direction you pull. Swipe diagonally to control the angle of your shot. The angle is crucial for ensuring that the thorn ball bounces effectively off walls and reaches the balloons in Thorn and Balloons.

### Release to Launch

Once you are satisfied with the strength and angle, let go to launch the thorn ball in Thorn and Balloons. The thorn ball falls under the influence of gravity and bounces when it hits the wall. Watch as realistic physics takes over, influencing the ball's trajectory and rebound patterns.

The goal of Thorn and Balloons is to destroy all balloons in the level. The balloon bursts when touched by the thorn ball. Pop all balloons to beat the level in Thorn and Balloons. If you miss or fail to pop them all, select the reset button on the fail screen to start over.
