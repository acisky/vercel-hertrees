Cheat or Repeat is a loose online puzzle game that tests your expectations and challenges how you think you learned. This game does not give you clear commands. Instead, it places you in simple conditions and lets you figure out what to do. Sometimes the answer is obvious. Other times, you must question everything and try something uncommon to succeed. This is what makes each play session unique.

## Unpredictability Drives Engagement

What makes this game interesting is its unpredictability. You might solve one level by repeating the same action. However, the next level may require a very different approach. The game often prompts you to overthink or follow patterns that do not work. This keeps every level fresh and surprising. Try to stay alert and adapt.

## Core Skills Required

Cheat or Repeat is not about fast reflexes. It is about observation, common sense, and creativity. You must be patient and willing to experiment. Even failing can help you learn the correct answer. Use each mistake as a clue.

## How to Play

Start by reading or looking at the level carefully before taking any action. Use mouse clicks or simple keys to interact with items or choices. Try the obvious solution first. If it fails, check different actions. Repeat movements when needed, but do not assume they will always work. Look for hidden clues or unusual details in every stage. Think differently—sometimes the "wrong" move is actually correct.

## Controls

- **Mouse Click** – Select options or interact with elements
- **Arrow Keys / WASD** – Navigate (in certain levels)
- **Spacebar / Enter** – Confirm actions or proceed

## Why Players Enjoy It

- Simple design but clever and complex puzzles
- Each level feels unique and unexpected
- Encourages creative thinking and experimentation
- Great for short play sessions yet still mentally engaging

## Best for Puzzle Fans

Cheat or Repeat is perfect for players who enjoy brain games that break the rules. It is easy to start, but solving each level can be surprisingly challenging and fun. Start playing and see how your expectations get tested.
