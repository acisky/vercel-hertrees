Slice Master 3D is a knife skill challenge game. Cut through every object you encounter along the path. Cut them into pieces and watch as they split perfectly in half. This is a truly impressive adventure where you explore vibrant scenes with a sharp knife in hand. Start playing and have fun!

## How To Play

Start with your character's sharp knife. This is your main tool to cut all items on the road. Slice Master 3D features a wide range of cuttable objects, including fruits, food, furniture, pillars, wooden bars, water pipes, and various cubic structures. Cut everything in your path without hesitation. With just one slash, you could split them cleanly in half.

### Controls

A simple click makes the knife jump and flip. Try to click repeatedly to keep the knife flying and moving forward. Each click adds one rotation, flip, and acceleration. You must time your movements precisely to make sure every object along the way gets cut.

### Scoring Tips

Scoring in Slice Master 3D depends on cutting as many objects as possible. Don't miss any object that helps you earn points. Also, avoid the red spikes at all costs — touching them means you lose. When you reach the finish line, try to land the knife on the scoring line to boost your final score. The score column has different levels that could keep, add, or subtract your money. To optimize your performance, you must time your adjustments correctly.
