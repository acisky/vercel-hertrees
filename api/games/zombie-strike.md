Zombie Strike is a fast-shooting game. This is a scenario where you are trapped in a base surrounded by aggressive pixel monsters. These creatures attempt to breach your defenses from all directions. You must protect your base against multiple waves of hungry undead. Try to locate hidden weapons scattered throughout the various rooms. You must eliminate every enemy to collect the money required for gear upgrades. The final objective is to defeat the bosses and save humanity.

## How to Play Zombie Strike

### Secure Base Perimeter
Zombie Strike enemies attack in groups that increase in speed and strength over time. This is why you must first earn money by killing adversaries to purchase advanced weapons from the shop. You must navigate through narrow passages and use the environment to block zombie paths. Press the E key to close doors, which could give you time to reload or locate health kits. You must explore every room to find secret items and superior equipment for future waves.

### Pro Survival Tactics
Zombie Strike soldiers must keep moving constantly to avoid being trapped in corners. Try to use knives or axes for single zombies to conserve your bullets. You must focus your fire on the heads of boss monsters to deal maximum damage. This is a rule to always shut doors behind you to slow down the chasing horde. You could save your flamethrower for large groups of weak enemies in confined spaces. You must upgrade your movement speed early to outrun the fastest zombie types.

### Game Controls
- WASD to move.
- Left mouse click to shoot.
- E to interact with doors or items.
- Keys 1 to 9 to switch weapons.

## Why This Pixel Shooter Is Intense

Zombie Strike creates a tense atmosphere through the use of tight 3D spaces. You must utilize a variety of weapons, which range from simple axes to massive machine guns. This pixel art style provides the intense combat with a classic and unique appearance. Zombie Strike offers a professional mix of fast shooting and strategic base management. Every successful wave could bring you closer to the final goal of time travel.
