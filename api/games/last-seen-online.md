## Curiosity and Discovery

You were always curious about other people's secrets, weren't you? This is exactly why you purchased an old computer from a pile of forgotten items at a garage sale. You didn't even take the time to format the hard drive before powering it on. Your curiosity took over completely. After all, a personal computer could be considered an extension of someone's soul, containing all their hidden secrets, personal projects, and private interactions with others.

## About "last seen online"

"last seen online" is a psychological horror escape room game that draws inspiration from classic early flash games. This experience requires you to carefully examine someone's computer files. You must solve challenging puzzles and uncover the mysterious secrets hidden within the digital files of this abandoned computer.
