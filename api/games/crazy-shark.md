## Crazy Shark Gameplay Guide

Crazy Shark presents a relentless survival challenge. You are either the hunter or become prey for others. This is a game that throws you into the ocean with an empty stomach. Your health bar dropping every second creates an invisible pressure that forces constant movement. Try to stay alive as the hunger drives your actions.

The survival mechanics in Crazy Shark are harsh but fair. More eating means longer life. However, the ocean contains many enemies in deep trenches. Many times you chase a school of fish and forget you enter larger creatures' territory. This shows the thin line between predator and prey. You must be brave and face the lurking dangers.

### FRENZY IN THE DEEP SEA

Initially, you start as a small shark. You must press and drag the mouse to weave around. This allows you to dodge bombs and spiky creatures, eat smaller fish and seashells, and gather gold coins and diamonds. To speed up, you could activate the right mouse button. When the gold coin bar at the bottom corner fills up, you win the level. Maintaining the health bar in the top right corner is crucial. If this bar depletes, you have to quit the game halfway through.

### Upgrades of Crazy Shark

Upgrades in Crazy Shark change the game when you accumulate enough experience. You can unlock a new larger shark species with more terrifying damage. Especially, you witness the real moment of explosion when the screen flares up. Your shark becomes invincible and can devour everything in its path. All frustration of dodging disappears, replaced by satisfaction from sweeping the map clean.

Not stopping there, you could equip your shark with jet engines or lasers. Must use heavy weapons to destroy more powerful prey.

### Companions and Combat Support

You don't have to fight alone in the deep sea. The game provides baby sharks to accompany you. They support your combat and hunting abilities.

### How to Hunt

Press and hold the left mouse button and drag to direct the shark to swallow creatures and dodge mines.
Click the right mouse button to increase swimming speed.

### Secrets to Dominate the Ocean

Manage your stamina bar wisely. Try to avoid greed and rush into minefields or sea urchin clusters. Prioritize collecting diamonds and coins to upgrade your stats as soon as possible. Eat small prey, build up strength, and wait for the opportunity to turn the tide.
